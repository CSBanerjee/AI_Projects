EMEA is our largest region by ARR contributing 41 percent of global revenue in FY2023.
Operated from London HQ with offices in Frankfurt, Paris, Amsterdam, Stockholm, and Dubai.
GDPR, national data sovereignty laws, and financial services regulation including MiFID II
and PSD2 create both compliance challenges and sales opportunities.
