KPI DEFINITIONS — COMMERCIAL ANALYTICS DIVISION
Version 2.1 | Owned by: Analytics Team | Last reviewed: January 2024

This document is the single source of truth for all commercial KPI definitions.

1. REVENUE METRICS

1.1 Average Selling Price (ASP)
  Definition: Total new business ACV divided by the number of new logos closed in the period.
  Formula: ASP = Total New ACV divided by Number of New Logos
  Frequency: Reported monthly, trended quarterly
  Exclusions: Renewals, expansions, and professional services revenue are excluded.
  Target FY2024: USD 145,000 company-wide
  Owner: VP of Sales

1.2 Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
  Definition: Annualised value of all active subscription contracts at a point in time.
  Formula: ARR = Sum of all active monthly recurring revenue multiplied by 12
  Target FY2024: USD 42,000,000
  Owner: Finance

1.3 Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
  Definition: Revenue retained from existing customers including expansions net of churn.
  Formula: NRR = Starting ARR plus Expansion ARR minus Churned ARR minus Contraction ARR divided by Starting ARR multiplied by 100
  Target FY2024: 110 percent or above
  Owner: VP of Customer Success

1.4 Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)
  Definition: Revenue retained from existing customers excluding expansion or upsell revenue.
  Formula: GRR = Starting ARR minus Churned ARR minus Contraction ARR divided by Starting ARR multiplied by 100
  Target FY2024: 88 percent or above
  Owner: VP of Customer Success

2. SALES EFFICIENCY METRICS

2.1 Win Rate
  Definition: Percentage of qualified opportunities resulting in closed won outcome.
  Formula: Win Rate = Closed Won divided by Closed Won plus Closed Lost multiplied by 100
  Scope: Opportunities reaching Stage 3 or beyond. Early withdrawals are excluded.
  Frequency: Weekly rolling 13 week and monthly point in time
  Target FY2024: 28 percent or above company-wide
  Regional targets: APAC 31 percent or above. EMEA 27 percent or above. North America 26 percent or above.
  Owner: VP of Sales

2.2 Pipeline Coverage Ratio
  Definition: Ratio of total pipeline value Stages 2 to 5 to remaining revenue target.
  Formula: Coverage = Open Pipeline ACV divided by Remaining Quota
  Minimum acceptable coverage: 3.0x at the start of a quarter
  Warning threshold: Below 2.5x triggers a pipeline review
  Critical threshold: Below 2.0x triggers a VP intervention
  Owner: Sales Operations

2.3 Quota Attainment
  Definition: Individual rep performance as actual closed won ACV divided by assigned quota.
  Formula: Closed Won ACV divided by Assigned Quota multiplied by 100
  Threshold: Reps below 70 percent for two consecutive quarters are placed on a performance improvement plan.
  Target distribution: 45 percent or more of reps above 100 percent. 25 percent or more at 80 to 100 percent.
  Owner: VP of Sales

2.4 Sales Cycle Length
  Definition: Average calendar days from Stage 1 creation to Closed Won.
  Target FY2024: 87 days or fewer
  Owner: Sales Operations

2.5 Magic Number
  Definition: How much ARR is generated for every dollar spent on Sales and Marketing.
  Formula: Net New ARR quarter multiplied by 4 divided by Sales and Marketing Spend prior quarter
  Target FY2024: 0.75 or above. Above 1.0 indicates highly efficient growth.
  Owner: CFO

3. CUSTOMER METRICS

3.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  Formula: Total Sales and Marketing Spend divided by New Logos
  Target FY2024: USD 48,000 or below. Payback period target 18 months or less.
  Owner: CFO and Marketing

3.2 Churn Rate (Logo Churn)
  Formula: Churned Customers divided by Customers at Period Start multiplied by 100
  Target FY2024: 8 percent or below annually
  Owner: VP of Customer Success

3.3 Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  Calculation: Percentage of Promoters scoring 9 to 10 minus percentage of Detractors scoring 0 to 6
  Target FY2024: 42 or above
  Survey cadence: Quarterly sent 90 days post go live and at each annual renewal.
  Owner: Customer Success

4. REPORTING CADENCE

  Daily: Closed pipeline and intraday activity via Salesforce
  Weekly: Pipeline review win loss coverage ratio Monday call
  Monthly: Full KPI pack distributed to leadership by 5th working day
  Quarterly: Board pack investor metrics forecast versus actual reconciliation

5. GLOSSARY

  ACV: Annual Contract Value
  ARR: Annual Recurring Revenue
  MRR: Monthly Recurring Revenue
  TCV: Total Contract Value used for multi year deals
  MQL: Marketing Qualified Lead
  SQL: Sales Qualified Lead
  ICP: Ideal Customer Profile
  NRR: Net Revenue Retention
  GRR: Gross Revenue Retention
