DISCOUNT POLICY — COMMERCIAL ANALYTICS DIVISION
Version 4.0 | Owned by: Finance | Last reviewed: January 2024

1. POLICY PURPOSE

This document sets out the maximum permitted discounts that a sales representative
may offer without escalating to management for approval. All discounts are expressed
as a reduction from the standard list price.

2. STANDARD DISCOUNT SCHEDULE BY DEAL SIZE

  Deal size USD 0 to 49,999: Maximum rep level discount 5 percent
  Deal size USD 50,000 to 149,999: Maximum rep level discount 10 percent
  Deal size USD 150,000 to 499,999: Maximum rep level discount 15 percent
  Deal size USD 500,000 to 999,999: Maximum rep level discount 18 percent
  Deal size USD 1,000,000 and above: Requires VP approval

These are hard ceilings at the rep level. Offering a discount above the applicable
tier without prior written approval is a policy violation.

3. REGIONAL ADJUSTMENTS

APAC Asia-Pacific
  Enterprise accounts USD 150,000 ACV and above: Standard tier plus 5 percent additional.
  Maximum APAC enterprise discount: 20 percent.
  Rationale: Competitive dynamics in Japan, South Korea, and Australia require additional flexibility.
  SMB accounts below USD 150,000 ACV: No regional adjustment. Standard schedule applies.

EMEA Europe Middle East Africa
  Strategic markets UK Germany France Nordics enterprise: Standard tier plus 3 percent.
  Maximum strategic EMEA discount: 18 percent.
  Rest of EMEA: Standard schedule applies.

North America
  No regional adjustment. Standard schedule applies.

LATAM Latin America
  All deal sizes: Standard tier plus 2 percent additional.

4. APPROVAL TIERS FOR ABOVE POLICY DISCOUNTS

  Up to rep level ceiling: Rep self-approve. No SLA.
  Ceiling plus up to 5 percent: Regional Sales Manager. 24 hours.
  Ceiling plus 5 to 10 percent: VP of Sales. 48 hours.
  Ceiling plus 10 to 15 percent: VP of Sales and CFO. 72 hours.
  Above ceiling plus 15 percent: CEO and CFO. 5 business days.

All approval requests above rep level must be submitted via the Salesforce Discount
Approval flow. Verbal approvals are not valid.

5. DISCOUNT STACKING RULES

  a) Volume plus Regional: Apply the higher of the two applicable discounts not both additively.

  b) Multi year prepay discount: This IS additive to the volume discount.
     2 year prepay: up to 5 percent additional
     3 year prepay: up to 8 percent additional

  c) End of quarter incentive: Finance may authorise up to 3 percent additional for deals
     closing in the final two weeks of a fiscal quarter.

  d) Competitive displacement: additional 2 percent is permitted where the prospect provides
     a copy of the competitor commercial proposal. Requires Regional Sales Manager sign-off.

Maximum combined discount under any circumstances: 35 percent.
Deals requiring above 35 percent must be escalated to the CEO.

6. PROFESSIONAL SERVICES DISCOUNTS

  Below USD 25,000: 0 percent no discount permitted
  USD 25,000 to 99,999: 5 percent
  USD 100,000 and above: 10 percent

7. RENEWAL DISCOUNT POLICY

  On time renewal 30 or more days before expiry: Standard schedule applies.
  Late renewal fewer than 30 days before expiry: No discount without VP approval.
  Annual uplift: CPI plus 3 percent. Reps may waive the uplift without approval.
  Any reduction below prior year price requires Manager approval.

8. POLICY COMPLIANCE AND AUDIT

Finance conducts a quarterly audit of all closed deals.

Progressive discipline for violations:
  1st violation: Formal written warning
  2nd violation: Discount authority reduced by one tier for 6 months
  3rd violation: Escalation to HR potential removal of discount authority
